# UF4 — Reactivation Broadcast

*The highest-ROI near-term lead source: warm past customers.*

## Flow
1. Build segment in [[F05-Customers-and-Accounts]]: locality + last-service > N months + **opted-in only**.
2. Pick approved AiSensy template → preview audience (with excluded/opt-out count visible).
3. Send within quiet-hours rules → deliveries logged.
4. Replies flow into [[F01-Unified-Lead-Inbox]] tagged `source=reactivation` → normal lead-to-cash loop.
5. Measure: replies, bookings, revenue per broadcast in [[F12-Reports-and-Analytics]].

## Guardrails
- Consent is a hard gate (TRAI/DND) — see [[F11-Followups-and-Automations]].
- Frequency cap: max 1 marketing message per customer per 30 days.
