# SOP — On-Site Service

## Steps
1. **Before starting:** greet, confirm the reported issue, tap Start in the job, capture **before** photos.
2. Run the full checklist — no skipped items; fails get a note + photo.
3. Explain findings in plain language **before** doing chargeable extra work; log approved extras in the job.
4. Finish → **after** photos → tidy the workspace ("no-mess guarantee").
5. Walk the customer through what was done; set expectations (warranty, next service due).
6. Complete the job → invoice → collect ([[SOP-Payment-Collection]]).

## Quality bars
- Checklist completion rate = 100%; photos on every job.
- Leave-behind: sticker/card with the emergency number.
