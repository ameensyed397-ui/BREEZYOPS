# SOP — Data & Consent

## PII
- Customer data lives only in Breezyops — never in personal WhatsApp exports, personal drives, or screenshots.
- Access is role-scoped; don't share logins. Departing staff → deactivate same day.

## Consent (TRAI/DND)
- Marketing sends require `opted_in=true`; capture consent at first contact ("Can we message you offers/reminders on WhatsApp?").
- Opt-out honoured instantly and permanently until re-consent.
- Transactional messages (booking confirmations, invoices) are always allowed; marketing is the gated class.
- Quiet hours 9pm–9am for marketing.

## AI disclosure
- Breezy must identify itself as an AI assistant in every channel (recorded on the lead).

## Backups
- Daily automated export verified weekly; restore drill quarterly.
