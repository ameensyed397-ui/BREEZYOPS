# SOP — B2B Survey & Quoting

## Survey visit
1. Book as a job typed `survey`; take issue photos + unit inventory (count, tonnage, type, age) per site.
2. Ask operational constraints: access hours, security, safety inductions.
3. Confirm decision process: who signs, budget cycle, **GST invoice required?**

## Quote
1. Build in [[F06-Quotes-and-Proposals]] from catalog + survey findings; per-site line items.
2. Include scope, exclusions, SLA response times, validity (14 days default).
3. Preview PDF → sanity check totals → send to the named decision maker.
4. Follow-up cadence: D+2 check-in, D+7 nudge, D+14 close-or-archive.
