# SOP — Lead Handling

## Standard
- **Respond within 30 minutes** (brand promise). Urgent flag → treat as now.
- Every lead gets an outcome: booked, snoozed (with date), or lost (with reason). Never left `new` > 4h.

## Steps
1. Open [[F01-Unified-Lead-Inbox]]; work top-down by SLA timer.
2. Verify phone + locality; confirm service need in customer's words.
3. Qualify: B2C → book directly. B2B signals (office, multiple units, VRF/VRV, "our building") → switch segment, ask sites + GST-invoice requirement.
4. Book slot; confirm on the customer's channel.
5. If unreachable: 2 attempts same day, 1 next day → then snooze 7 days with note.

## Don'ts
- Don't quote firm prices for unseen complex work — book a survey.
- Don't mark lost without a reason code (feeds ROI reporting).
