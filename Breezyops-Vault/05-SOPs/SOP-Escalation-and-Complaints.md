# SOP — Escalation & Complaints

1. Complaint arrives (any channel) → log against the customer + job.
2. Acknowledge within 2h; never argue on chat — offer a call/revisit.
3. Pull the job's checklist + photos (evidence base) before responding on substance.
4. Resolution ladder: revisit free-of-charge → partial credit → refund (admin approval).
5. Root-cause note on the job; if a pattern, update the relevant SOP/checklist.
6. After resolution: do NOT send the standard review ask; send a "thanks for your patience" note instead.
