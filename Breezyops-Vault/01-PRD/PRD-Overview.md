# PRD — Breezyops Overview

## 1. Problem
Breezyair's operations are scattered across Notion, WhatsApp, spreadsheets, Zoho and Asad's head. Leads arrive on three channels (voice, WhatsApp, web chat via the **Breezy** agent) with no single queue. There is no owned system tracking a job from lead → service → invoice → retention, and no unified place for documents, photos, contracts and AMC schedules. B2B and B2C are run identically despite completely different cycles.

## 2. Vision
One **self-owned** operations platform — Breezyops — where every lead, appointment, job, customer, contract, invoice, document and photo lives in one place, for both B2B and B2C, with role-based access, automations and analytics. No third-party ops SaaS (no Zoho). Data, logic and storage are ours.

## 3. Goals
1. **Zero lead leakage** — all leads (incl. Breezy agent) land in one inbox with source attribution.
2. **Full lifecycle tracking** — separate B2C and B2B pipelines to invoice + retention.
3. **Owned invoicing & documents** — in-house invoice generation (pre-GST, GST-ready), per-client document & photo repository.
4. **AMC as the moat** — subscriptions auto-schedule visits and never miss a renewal.
5. **Lean cost** — launch entirely on free tiers; self-hostable later.
6. **Operational discipline** — SOPs, checklists and templates baked into the tool.

## 4. Non-goals (v1)
- Native mobile apps (use a mobile-first PWA instead).
- Full accounting / GST filing (invoicing only until registration).
- Multi-city / franchise support.
- Customer self-service portal (Phase 3 candidate).
