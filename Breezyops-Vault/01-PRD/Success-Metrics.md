# Success Metrics

## North-star
**Jobs completed per month** and **AMC active subscriptions** — volume + recurring revenue.

## Funnel / ops KPIs
| Metric | Target signal |
|---|---|
| Lead response time | Median < 30 min (brand promise) |
| Lead → booked conversion | Trend up; segment by source |
| Booked → completed | > 90% |
| Same-day emergency fulfilment | Track % of urgent-flagged leads served same day |
| AMC renewal rate | > 80% |
| Invoice → paid time | Median < 24h (B2C), within terms (B2B) |
| Source ROI | Cost per lead & per closed job by channel |
| Technician utilisation | Jobs/day, travel vs on-site time |
| Review capture rate | Reviews requested vs left (feeds local SEO) |
